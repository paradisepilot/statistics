\textbf{\large Abstract}
\end{center}
In these notes, we present an exposition of
the framework of SEVANI
(System for the Estimation of Variance due to Nonresponse and Imputation) \cite{Beaumont2011},
developed by Statistics Canada.
More precisely, we begin in Section \ref{sectionOperationObjecgive}
with a description of the operational objective of SEVANI.
In Section \ref{sectionSEVANITheory}, we present the underlying theory
of the SEVANI framework as a series of lemmas.
